Carbon Audit & Monitoring

We support entities and individuals to prepare project designs for the projects and activities.

This also enables them to register their projects for certified emissions reductions (CERs) awards by the Carbon Credit registry....

We conduct independent carbon audits on the emission status of companies and organizations.

This enables corporate firms to understand their carbon budget from their business operations and value chains.

It covers individuals (company executives, officers, etc), projects, operations, value chains, products, offices, and events, and presents appropriate carbon labels for its value chains and operation niches.

We support verified, certified and issued carbon credits to be sold and retired through the global carbon market exchange.

MRV Process

Africa Carbon Market Inc. supports entities to do the following: ..

1. PDD development: Support our clients in documentation, preparation, and submission of a detailed project design document (PDD), on the methodology for calculating its emission reductions;

2. Validation: Conducts independent audit to validate the project's carbon reduction capacity from implementation

3. Registration: Register and receive approval from the host country Credit Register to validate such a project for low carbon or emission reduction awards.

4. Monitoring: Monitor and quantify emissions generation based on the project design document (PDD), to document produced real, measurable, additional, and verifiable emission reduction.

5. Verification: Submit quantified emission reduction documentation to the credit registry (Board).
