About Us

Africa Carbon Market Inc. is a global platform for climate change adaptation and mitigation transactions through compliance and voluntary market pathways. It presents high-level technical and business opportunities to reduce emissions and create a decarbonization pathway for all actors presenting project developers and emission offset market players to interface. The company’s corporate interest is to enhance carbon-removing projects and entities to get on both the compliance (regulatory) and voluntary carbon markets to mobilize green funds through both climate finance and the issuance and trade of standard certified carbon credits on the market. It additionally provides a standard infrastructure for compliance and voluntary market players, to ensure globally verified offset supply (sellers) and off-set demand (buyers) to digitally transact and manage credits from issuance to retirement.

Mission

Africa Carbon Market Inc. will be the unique platform for strategic professional services in climate and carbon management in individual, corporate, and project carbon accounting for both compliance and voluntary carbon markets and facilitate their access to climate finance or financial earnings from emissions avoidance or reduction operations and projects.

Vision

To be Africa’s go-to offset market platform and a global platform for verified offset trade, climate finance, green projects, and climate change management.

Business Focus

• Low/Zero-carbon (Green) /Projects (Design and Development)

• Green Technologies and Solutions Deployment.

• Carbon Footprint Advisory (Corporate, Individual, and Project)

• Consultancy (Climate Change Management and Low Carbon Investments)

• Carbon Offsets and Carbon Credit Exchange Trade.
